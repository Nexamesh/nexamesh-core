## Executive Summary

Nexamesh (formerly NexaMesh) operates two product lines sharing a core
AI + blockchain detection platform: **NexaMesh Kestrel** for consumer sports
training and **NexaMesh Sentinel** for enterprise infrastructure security.

The software platform is complete and functional. Hardware is in early R&D —
toy quadcopter test platform, flight controller on order, salvaged motors being
assessed. This plan is built from today's real position.

### 12-Month Objectives (Q2 2026 – Q2 2027)

- Secure pre-seed funding (TIA Seed Fund, cloud credits, accelerator)
- Build basic hardware detection prototype (F405 + GPS + detector on Jetson)
- Record field detection demo video
- First external customer conversation (ARMSCOR or CSIR DPSS)
- SA Pty Ltd incorporation and CSD registration
